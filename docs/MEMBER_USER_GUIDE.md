## Getting Started

### What is the HOA Management System?

The Sanderson Creek HOA Management System is your one-stop portal for:
- Staying informed about community announcements
- Viewing and registering for community events
- Accessing important HOA documents
- Participating in community discussions
- Managing your account information

### System Requirements

- **Browsers:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection:** Broadband recommended
- **Screen Resolution:** 1024x768 or higher

## Registration and Login

### Creating Your Account

![Registration Page](../frontend/screenshots/03-registration-page.png)

1. **Navigate to the Registration Page**
   - Go to the HOA portal website
   - Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** link

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - **Full Name:** Enter your legal name as it appears in HOA records
   - **Email Address:** Use a valid email you check regularly
   - **Password:** Create a strong password with:
     - At least 8 characters
     - One uppercase letter
     - One lowercase letter
     - One number
     - One special character
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter your password

3. **Complete CAPTCHA Verification**
   - Complete the Cloudflare Turnstile challenge to verify you're human

4. **Submit Registration**
   - Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"** button
   - You'll receive a confirmation message

5. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - Your account will be activated once verified

6. **Admin Approval**
   - After email verification, an administrator will review and approve your account
   - You'll receive an email notification once approved
   - This process typically takes 1-2 business days

### Logging In

![Login Page](../frontend/screenshots/01-login-page.png)

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**
   - Go to the HOA portal website
   - Click **"Sign In"** or **"Login"**

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - **Email:** Enter your registered email address
   - **Password:** Enter your password

3. **Click "Sign In"**
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Form Validation

![Login Validation Errors](../frontend/screenshots/02-login-validation-errors.png)

The system validates your input and will display helpful error messages if:
- Required fields are empty
- Email format is invalid
- Password doesn't meet requirements

### Forgot Password?

![Forgot Password Page](../frontend/screenshots/04-forgot-password-page.png)

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the reset link in the email
6. Enter your new password (must meet security requirements)
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click **"Reset Password"**
9. You'll be redirected to login with your new password

Announcements keep you informed about important community news, updates, and notices.

1. Click **"Announcements"** in the navigation menu

### Email Notifications

- Important announcements may be sent to your email
- Check your email regularly for critical updates
- You can update your email preferences in your profile

## Accessing Documents

![Member Documents Page](../frontend/screenshots/10-member-documents-page.png)

The document library contains important HOA documents, forms, and resources.

### Viewing Documents

1. Click **"Documents"** in the navigation menu
2. Browse the list of available documents

### Document Information

Each document shows:
- **Title:** Document name
- **Description:** Brief description of contents
- **File Type:** PDF, Word, Excel, etc.
- **File Size:** Size of the document
- **Upload Date:** When it was added
- **Status:** Approved documents are available for download

### Downloading Documents

1. **Find the Document**
   - Use search to find specific documents
   - Browse by category or date

2. **Click "Download" or the Document Title**
   - Document will download to your device
   - Check your Downloads folder

3. **Open the Document**
   - Use appropriate application (PDF reader, Word, etc.)

### Document Types

Common documents include:
- HOA Bylaws and CC&Rs
- Community Guidelines
- Architectural Review Forms
- Meeting Minutes
- Annual Budgets and Financial Reports
- Community Maps and Plans
- Contact Lists
- Event Information

### Document Security

- Only approved documents are visible to members
- Some documents may be restricted to certain user groups
- Do not share sensitive HOA documents publicly

## Community Discussions

![Member Discussions List](../frontend/screenshots/11-member-discussions-list.png)

The discussion forum allows community members to communicate, share ideas, and collaborate.

### Viewing Discussions

1. Click **"Discussions"** in the navigation menu
2. Browse all discussion threads

### Discussion Features

Each discussion thread shows:
- **Title:** Topic of discussion
- **Author:** Member who started the discussion
- **Date Created:** When the thread was started
- **Number of Replies:** How many responses the thread has received
- **Preview:** First few lines of the discussion content

### Creating a New Discussion

![Create Discussion Dialog](../frontend/screenshots/12-member-create-discussion-dialog.png)

1. **Click "New Discussion" or "Create Discussion"**
2. **Fill in the Form:**
   - **Title:** Give your discussion a clear, descriptive title
   - **Content:** Write your message or question
     - Be respectful and constructive
     - Stay on topic
     - Follow community guidelines
3. **Click "Create" or "Post"**
4. Your discussion will appear in the forum

### Replying to Discussions

1. **Click on a discussion thread** to open it
2. Read the original post and existing replies
3. **Click "Reply" or scroll to the reply form**
4. **Write Your Response:**
   - Be respectful and constructive
   - Add value to the conversation
   - Stay on topic
5. **Click "Submit" or "Post Reply"**

### Discussion Guidelines

- **Be Respectful:** Treat other members with courtesy
- **Stay On Topic:** Keep discussions relevant to the community
- **No Personal Attacks:** Focus on ideas, not individuals
- **Protect Privacy:** Don't share personal information
- **Report Issues:** Contact administrators if you see inappropriate content

### Managing Your Discussions

- **Edit:** You can edit your discussions and replies
- **Delete:** You can delete your own posts
- **Notifications:** You may receive notifications about replies (check your profile settings)

## Managing Your Profile

![Member Profile Page](../frontend/screenshots/13-member-profile-page.png)

Your profile contains your account information and settings.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click **"Profile"** in the navigation menu
2. Or click your name/avatar in the top navigation bar

### Profile Information

View and update:
- **Name:** Your full name
- **Email Address:** Contact email
- **Account Status:** Approved, Pending, or Rejected
- **Member Since:** Your registration date
- **Role:** Member status

### Updating Your Information

1. **Click "Edit Profile" or "Update Information"**
2. **Modify Your Details:**
   - Change your name
   - Update email address (requires verification)
3. **Click "Save Changes"**

### Changing Your Password

1. **Navigate to Profile Page**
2. **Click "Change Password"**
3. **Fill in the Form:**
   - **Current Password:** Enter your existing password
   - **New Password:** Create a new strong password
   - **Confirm New Password:** Re-enter new password
4. **Click "Update Password"**
5. You'll receive a confirmation message

### Password Requirements

Your new password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter (A-Z)
- One lowercase letter (a-z)
- One number (0-9)
- One special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Account Security

- **Use a Strong Password:** Don't reuse passwords from other sites
- **Change Regularly:** Update your password every 3-6 months
- **Don't Share:** Never share your password with anyone
- **Log Out:** Always log out when using shared computers

## Security and Privacy

### Your Data Protection

The Sanderson Creek HOA takes your privacy seriously:

- **Secure Login:** Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- **HTTPS:** All data transmission is encrypted
- **Limited Access:** Only approved members can access the portal
- **Data Privacy:** Your personal information is not shared with third parties

### Best Practices

1. **Strong Passwords**
   - Use unique passwords
   - Include letters, numbers, and special characters
   - Don't share passwords

2. **Email Security**
   - Use a secure email address
   - Don't click suspicious links in emails
   - Verify emails claiming to be from the HOA

3. **Device Security**
   - Keep your devices updated
   - Use antivirus software
   - Lock your devices when not in use

4. **Logging Out**
   - Always log out when finished
   - Especially on shared or public computers
   - Clear browser cache on public computers

### Reporting Security Issues

If you notice suspicious activity or security concerns:
1. Log out immediately
2. Contact the HOA administrator
3. Change your password
4. Report the issue via email to: info@sandersoncreekhoa.com

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### I Can't Log In

**Problem:** Login fails or shows error message

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your email and password**
   - Ensure caps lock is off
   - Check for typos
   - Email addresses are case-insensitive

2. **Account not verified**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Resend verification email if needed

3. **Account pending approval**
   - Wait for administrator approval
   - Contact HOA office if it's been more than 2 business days

4. **Forgot password**
   - Use "Forgot Password" link
   - Follow reset instructions

#### I Didn't Receive the Verification Email

**Solutions:**
1. **Check spam/junk folder**
2. **Wait a few minutes** - emails may be delayed
3. **Click "Resend Verification Email"**
4. **Verify email address is correct**
5. **Contact administrator** if issue persists

#### Pages Are Loading Slowly

**Solutions:**
1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Clear browser cache and cookies**
3. **Try a different browser**
4. **Disable browser extensions**
5. **Restart your browser or device**

#### I Can't Download Documents

**Solutions:**
1. **Check browser download settings**
2. **Disable popup blockers**
3. **Try right-click and "Save As"**
4. **Ensure sufficient disk space**
5. **Try a different browser**

#### Error Messages

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
2. **Clear browser cache**
3. **Log out and log back in**
4. **Try incognito/private browsing mode**
5. **Contact administrator with error details**

### Getting Help

If you continue to experience issues:

1. **Document the Problem:**
   - What were you trying to do?
   - What happened instead?
   - What error messages did you see?
   - Take screenshots if possible

2. **Contact Support:**
   - Email: info@sandersoncreekhoa.com
   - Phone: (555) 123-4567
   - Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

3. **Include Information:**
   - Your name and email address
   - Browser and version
   - Device type (computer, tablet, phone)
   - Operating system (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android)
   - Screenshots of error messages
